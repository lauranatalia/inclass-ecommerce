The Tyrannousaurus is the most fierce of all dinosaurs!

The Tyrannousaurus lived during the [Late Cretaceous Period.](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tyrannosaurus)

## Features

- Big teeth 
- Little arms
- Thick tail
